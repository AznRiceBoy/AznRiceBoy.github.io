The Different Forms of Ice 

                            The dictionary definition of Ice is “frozen water, a brittle, transparent crystalline solid”.This, however,
                        is not always the case. I have explored the world for approximately 18 years and have gathered an extensive
                        amount of information on the subject of “Ice”. This webpage is my way of sharing my knowledge on the different
                        types of Ice one can find while exploring the world.

                        I believe Ice could be split into 3 different categories:
                          1. The solid form of water. A.k.a. “Cold Bois”
                          2. The form of Jewelry, commonly referring to gold objects surrounded by diamonds.
                          3. The accessory called watches. This is my personal favorite form of ice. Some people know this
                          form of ice to be called “Heat”.
